WHAT THE SPEEDSTER RACE SCHOOL DOES

Speedster Racing Academy caters to both novice and seasoned drivers, offering comprehensive race and high-performance driver education. Our programs not only elevate racing skills but also serve as a pathway to licensing with various national organizations, including our exclusive Speedster Racing Academy events.
Opt for our "All-Inclusive" package, where we provide everything you need, eliminating concerns about equipment compatibility. Our dedicated team of expert instructors ensures you focus on driving and learning, not vehicle maintenance. With meticulously prepared high-performance cars, including our signature models, we guarantee an immersive experience on and off the track.


Join the Speedster Racing Academy today and embark on a thrilling journey to hone your racing skills and achieve your licensing goals. No worries, just pure driving enjoyment!

RACE SCHOOL PROGRAM

Speedster Racing Academy provides enthusiastic learners with a complete package, including a race car, safety gear (such as helmets and race suits if needed), fuel, fluids, tires, lunch, on-track and classroom instruction, a framed certificate upon successful completion, and professional photography services by our skilled photographer. To ensure a personalized and focused learning experience, class sizes are deliberately limited to five students.

Our students take the wheel of race-ready Spec Racer Ford (SRF) Gen 2 mid-engine cars, featuring a resilient 1.9L four-cylinder engine paired with a five-speed manual transmission. These SRFs adhere to strict specifications, emphasizing driver ability and improvement.

Meet our Chief Instructor, Scott Stephens, whose extensive on-track and road racing experience, combined with in-depth knowledge of SpecRacer Ford construction and performance, ensures top-tier training. Scott, a key figure since the school's inception in 1985, has worked alongside his father, race school founder Mike Stephens, and currently serves as the track owner, manager, and Customer Service Rep for SCCA Enterprises and SpecRacer Ford.

Attending Speedster Racing Academy costs $1,600 per person per day, with a 50% deposit due at registration and the remainder one day before the event. We take pride in offering exceptional value with competitive pricing, delivering more track time than other programs. Start a conversation today and accelerate your racing aspirations with Speedster Racing Academy.

8:30-9:30AM: Introductions, Classroom, Driver/Car setup

ABOUT THE SPEEDSTER RACE SHOP

Nestled amid the undulating landscapes and serpentine tracks of the Speedster Racing Circuit, the Speedster Academy Garage stands as a comprehensive automotive and mechanical facility. Specializing in the crafting, upkeep, and assistance of race cars and high-performance vehicles, our garage caters to a spectrum of needs. Whether you seek a complete build for the Spec Racer Ford (SRF) Generation Three, a meticulous corner balance, race coaching, or on-site support, the Speedster Academy Garage is your gateway to professional service and unwavering assistance.


As one of the exclusive Customer Service Representatives for Sports Car Club of America's (SCCA) Enterprises, Speedster Racing Academy stands out in providing unparalleled support for your SRF, race car, or high-performance vehicle. Our cutting-edge Speedster Racing Academy Workshop is equipped with over 5,600 square feet of workspace, staffed by skilled technicians, featuring multiple working bays. With an exceptional level of expertise, we are well-equipped to address your specific motorsports needs, ensuring the optimal maintenance, building, and support of your racing machines.

HISTORY

The Speedster Racing Workshop, under the guidance of the Stephens family and directed by Scott Stephens with over three decades of experience in the motorsports realm, stands as a premier facility in the nation. Collaborating with his family and skilled technicians, Scott has played a pivotal role in establishing the Speedster Racing Workshop as a top-tier destination for the meticulous care of race cars and high-performance vehicles. Their track record includes successful campaigns with a diverse range of vehicles, from Pro Formula Mazdas and Spec Miatas to SRFs and Porsche 911 GT3 Cup cars.


Over the years, Speedster Racing Academy's service offerings have transformed, expanding from fundamental maintenance to comprehensive major and minor repairs, meticulous vehicle setups, thorough preparation, efficient transportation, personalized coaching, on-site trackside assistance, tire installations, "Arrive & Drive" programs, and a myriad of additional services. For more than three decades, Speedster Racing Academy has been a guiding force, assisting both novice and experienced drivers in realizing their track aspirations.
